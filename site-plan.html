Overview

Purpose

I like fly fishing but I’m an avid fly-tying enthusiast. I’m also learning to build websites. What better way to combine these to things to create a web site the showcases this rare art form? I want to build a site dedicated to sharing the creative and artistic side of fly-tying. A place where people like myself can admire these little masterpieces and get inspired. This site would also be a place where we could share patterns, tips, tricks and techniques to help keep this art form alive.

Audience

People interested in Fly Tying or want to learn more about fly tying

Typography

Heading Font: Amatic SC

I chose Amatic SC as it reminds my of my hand writing and I think it adds to the handcrafted feel that comes with fly tying.

Paragraph Font: Oswald 'light'

I chose Oswald 'light' becasue it was a suggested pairing from Google Fonts and I think it is easy to read.
